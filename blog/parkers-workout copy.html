Finding My Ideal Routine

January 1, 2024

All exercises will be performed in the 6-10 rep range unless stated otherwise. Start with a heavy weight that you can only rep for 6. Next time you work that muscle group, try for more reps. Repeat until you can easily hit 10 reps. Then, up the weight and go back down to 6 reps. This is called progressive overload.
